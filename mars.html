The red planet

Mars is called the "Red Planet." Scientists think it might have had water long ago.Mars, the fourth planet from the Sun, is often called the "Red Planet" due to its rusty, reddish appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface. It's a terrestrial planet, meaning it's rocky like Earth, and has a thin atmosphere primarily composed of carbon dioxide. Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, and is known for its extreme temperatures, large dust storms, and the tallest volcano in our solar system, Olympus Mons.
