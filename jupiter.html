The Giant Planet

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.

It has a massive storm called the Great Red Spot and at least 79 moons. Jupiter is the fifth and largest planet from the Sun, a gas giant composed primarily of hydrogen and helium, and is famous for its striped appearance, the persistent Great Red Spot storm, and its numerous moons. It has a powerful magnetic field and a complex system of thin, dusty rings, (that is not visible from earth)and its immense gravity influences the dynamics of the entire solar system by attracting comets and asteroids. Jupiter have the most moon in the solar system.
